Our Story

A Movement Rooted in Compassion & Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation, licensed by the Central Government, with its base in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

The Foundation holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability and tax-exempt benefits for donors. It is also CSR-1 registered, enabling corporate partnerships, and NITI Aayog registered, aligning its work with national development goals. The prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our commitment to quality standards.

With a strong network of dedicated professionals, volunteers and corporate partners across 28 states, InAmigos Foundation works tirelessly to bring positive, measurable change through food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability and social inclusion programs across India.

The name "InAmigos" is derived from a blend of inspiration and the Spanish word for friends — because we believe that true change begins with friendship, solidarity, and a shared will to act.

Project SEVA

Food & Clothing for the Underprivileged

Tackling hunger and inadequate clothing among impoverished communities, Project Seva provides nutritious meals and essential clothing. Beyond meeting basic needs, it fosters dignity and raises awareness about food security, ensuring families don't go to bed hungry or suffer in extreme weather conditions.
